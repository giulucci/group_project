About Us


We are fellow MBA students!

After spending our first course selection cycle wrestling with the EBS website, we decided to take matters into our own hands!

We built LBS Scheduler using technology, a little bit of coding knowledge, and many hours on Google.
This tool is made to help any student spend more time curating their courses and less time strugling to fit everything in the week.
